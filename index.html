Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our pressure washing services

How much does pressure washing cost in Hudson, FL?

Pressure washing costs vary based on the service. Driveway cleaning typically ranges from $100-$250, house washing from $200-$500, and pool deck cleaning from $150-$350. We provide free, no-obligation quotes for all services. Bundle multiple services to save!

Is soft washing safe for my home's siding?

Yes, soft washing is completely safe for siding, stucco, and brick. Unlike high-pressure washing, soft washing uses low pressure combined with specialized cleaning solutions to safely remove mold, mildew, and algae without risk of damage to your home's exterior.

Are you licensed and insured?

What areas do you serve?

We serve all of Pasco and Hernando Counties including Hudson, New Port Richey, Spring Hill, Port Richey, Trinity, Land O' Lakes, Holiday, Shady Hills, and Elfers. Contact us to confirm service in your area.

How often should I have my house pressure washed in Florida?

In Florida's humid climate, we recommend having your home's exterior soft washed every 12-18 months to prevent mold, mildew, and algae buildup. Driveways and pool decks may need cleaning every 6-12 months depending on usage and shade coverage.
